ABOUT

Samskruti College of Engineering and Technology was established in 2005 by St.Vincent Educational Society and is affiliated to JNTU, Hyderabad. The college is a reputed institution in the State of Telangana and has 14 years of existence. Samskruti College offers courses like BTech, MTech and MBA at the UG and the PG level. Candidates need to go through an entrance examination to get admission in the college. The Training and Placement Cell of Samskruti College functions with the sole aim of placing students in better companies just before they complete their courses. Some of the top companies that recruit students from the college include Accenture, TCS, IBM, Wipro, CTS, Infosys, SIFY, L&T, Siemens, Marshall Technologies, CSS Corporation, Spur technologies etc.
